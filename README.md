# Poop Diary

Poop Diary is a fun, intuitive health tracker for monitoring bowel movements. Using the Bristol Stool Chart, you can log frequency, difficulty, and duration with our cute mascot, Pipi. Features beautiful monthly habit grids, weekly consistency trends, and helpful gut health tips. Track your way to better wellness!

## Features

- **Logging**: Quickly log your bowel movements using the standardized Bristol Stool Chart icons.
- **Stats**: Visualize your gut health with:
  - **Monthly Habit Grid**: A color-coded calendar showing frequency and the most common stool type per day.
  - **Type Consistency**: A segmented bar chart showing your trends over the last 7 days.
  - **Weekly Overview**: A simple Sunday-to-Saturday summary.
- **Pipi Mascot**: A friendly companion that guides you through the app and reacts to your logs.

## Tech Stack

- **SwiftUI**: Modern, declarative UI framework.
- **Core Data**: Robust local persistence for your health data.
- **Performance**: High-speed image caching for a flicker-free, fluid experience.

## Getting Started

1. Open `PoopDiary.xcodeproj` in Xcode.
2. Select your target device or simulator.
3. Build and run (⌘ + R).
